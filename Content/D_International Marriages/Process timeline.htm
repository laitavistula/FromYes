Process timeline

Process timeline for international documents:

Pre-marriage (Start 6+ months in advance)

  • Foreigners must submit their documents (e.g., birth certificates, proof of identity, marital status confirmation) for translation and authentication.

Post-marriage - registration:

  • Once the ceremony is completed, the international couple must register their marriage with the local USC.

A fee is required for the issuance of the marriage certificate. Payment methods and additional fees should be confirmed directly with the USC.

 

Check your home country’s regulations to ensure the marriage is legally recognized abroad.
