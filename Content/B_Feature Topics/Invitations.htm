Invitations

Wedding invitations set the tone and provide essential details like dress code, venue directions, and parking info.

  • Traditional approach – In Poland, close family and friends are most often invited in person, with senior guests receiving their invitations first.

  • Digital options – Paper invitations remain popular, but email or wedding websites offer a budget-friendly and eco-conscious alternative.

  • RSVP tracking – Online forms or spreadsheets simplify guest list management and ensure accurate headcounts.

Tip: If inviting international guests, consider bilingual invitations to avoid confusion.
