Humanist weddings

A humanist wedding is a non-religious ceremony offering personalized vows and rituals. It is symbolic, and a legal civil marriage must occur before or after the humanist ceremony.

  • Complete the civil marriage process at the registry office before or after the humanist ceremony.

  • Humanist ceremonies are customizable and can take place at various locations.

  • The celebrant does not register the marriage; legal formalities must be handled separately.
