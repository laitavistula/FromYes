Humanist wedding (ślub humanistyczny)

Overview

  • Complete the civil marriage process at the Register office (Urząd Stanu Cywilnego, USC) before or after the humanist ceremony.

  • Humanist ceremonies are customizable and can take place at various locations.

  • The celebrant does not register the marriage; legal formalities must be handled separately.

  • A non-religious, personalized ceremony focusing on the couple's values and beliefs.

  • Not legally binding in Poland.

  • Often chosen by secular couples or those seeking a unique celebration.

  • Couples must also have a civil ceremony for legal recognition.
